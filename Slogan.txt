Objective
 
We want to understand the annual breakdown of shortages in the provided dataset.
 
Key Definitions
            	1.        	Shortage: A shortage occurs when Amazon believes that the supplier did not send the full inventory ordered to Amazon. As a result, Amazon will only pay part of the invoice
            	•         	Example: If Amazon ordered 100 units, but the supplier only sent 90, Amazon would not pay for the missing 10 units. This means there is a 10-unit shortage on that specific purchase order (PO) and invoice.


            	2.        	Shortage Categories:
            	•         	Current Shortages: Shortages that occurred within the last 90 calendar days past the due date.
            	•         	Aged Shortages: Shortages that are older than 90 calendar days past the due date.
 
Context of This Analysis

Our ultimate goal is to ensure our client gets paid for everything they have sent. The higher the recovered payment amount, the better job we have done.
